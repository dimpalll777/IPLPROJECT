Hardik Pandya!

Introduction

Hardik Pandya is one of India's best all-rounders. He is famous for explosive batting, fast bowling, athletic fielding, and confident leadership. Hardik has played an important role for Mumbai Indians and the Indian cricket team by winning many important matches.
